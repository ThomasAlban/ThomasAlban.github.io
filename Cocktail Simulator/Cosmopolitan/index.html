How to make a Cosmopolitan Cocktail

Lipsmackingly sweet and sour, the Cosmopolitan cocktail of vodka, cranberry, orange liqueur and citrus is a good-time in a glass. Perfect for a party

Step 1

Add 45ml of lemon vodka, 15ml of triple sec, 30ml of cranberry juice and 10ml of lime juice to a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a cocktail glass.

Step 2

To make the garnish: hold a 3cm round piece of orange zest about 10cm above your cocktail, and very carefully wave it over a lit match or lighter flame. Bend the outer edge of the zest in towards the flame so that the orange oils are released, then drop the zest into your drink.



Done!
