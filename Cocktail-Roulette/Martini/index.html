How to make a Martini Cocktail

Our version of the ultimate classic cocktail is stirred, not shaken. Serve your martini with a twist of lemon, or an olive

Step 1

Stir 60ml of gin or vodka, 1 tbsp of dry vermouth and a little ice together or put them in a cocktail shaker to combine.

Step 2

Strain into a chilled martini glass. Serve with an olive, on a cocktail stick, or with a twist of lemon peel.
